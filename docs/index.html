Intro.

My name is Ephrem Tadesse. I’m Graduate of information technology with specialization in machine learning and natural language processing at Jimma University in 2017. I have gained distinctive academic results and hands-on research experience. I have over 4 years of research and teaching experience at Jimma university, faculty of computing and Informatics. I have been working a research on Natural Language Processing(NLP) and Information extraction tasks. One of the prominent information extraction task; which I have conducted so far is “event and temporal information extraction from unstructured Amharic text”. Amharic is highly underrepresented Semitic language; thus my task was not only developing a model but also building corpora for the development, training and test purpose. I would say that I am strongly familiar with knowledge engineering approaches and machine learning algorithms. Most recently, I am also passionate about machine translation and Natural language interface for databases using sequence to sequence models such as Long short term memory(LSTM) networks. I have mentioned some of my publication and under publications link.

During my stay at jimma university; I have been involved in some automation projects and volunteer activities. One of my favorite project was focusing on the healthcare industries quality improvement automation system. The project has been developed using java with several features such as record management, website, and recommendation of healthcare organizations for end users based on users point of interest. Beside this, I was a facilitator and founder for “Code to High school students” volunteer program. Moreover, I am familiar with various programming languages, frameworks and data science tools such as pandas, Numpy, Matplotlib and scipy.
